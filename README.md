## What Makes This Different

Every habit app is a checkbox with a streak counter. RoutineOS has three things none of them have:

- **Adaptive Recovery Engine** — misses 2+ days? AI rebuilds your routine gradually. No guilt. No reset.
- **Life Mode Switching** — one tap switches to Exam / Travel / Sick mode. Routine adjusts automatically.
- **Consistency Score** — not a streak. A weighted % that accounts for how hard your week actually was.

## Features

### Smart Routine Builder
- Input wake time, sleep time, and schedule
- Pick top 3 goals
- Gemini AI generates a realistic daily routine
- Preview and edit before confirming

### Adaptive Recovery Engine
- Detects 2+ consecutive missed days automatically
- Asks why — exam, travel, sick, or other
- Generates a 3-day graduated recovery plan (40% → 70% → 100%)
- No streaks broken. Just a rebuild.

### Consistency Score
- Weighted completion % across last 30 days
- Mode-aware: missing during Exam Mode costs less than Normal Mode
- Shown as an animated circular progress ring
- Calculated nightly — always accurate

### Life Mode Switcher
- 5 modes: Normal / Exam / Travel / Sick / Grind
- Each mode shows a different subset of habits
- Set duration — auto-reverts after it ends
- Routine updates the moment you switch

### Morning Briefing
- Personalised 5-line message every morning at your wake time
- Based on your last 7 days, today's habits, and current mode
- Delivered as a push notification via Firebase FCM
- Generated by Gemini — never a generic message

### Context-Aware Reminders
- Habit reminders adapt based on your streak and recent pattern
- "You've been consistent 5 days — don't break it now"
- "Even 10 minutes counts today" — when you're on a recovery plan
- Dynamic messages, not static alerts

### Weekly Pattern Insights
- Every Sunday — AI analyses your last 7 days
- Shows best day, worst day, top habit, and what's slipping
- Written in plain language — not a data dump
- Displayed with completion charts and trend graphs

### Micro Habit Suggester
- Habit missed 3+ times in 7 days? AI suggests a 2-minute version
- "Can't do 30-min run" → "Run to the gate and back"
- Offered as an alternative before you skip
- Builds the habit pathway even on low-motivation days

### Accountability Buddy
- Invite one friend via 6-digit code
- See each other's consistency score only — habits stay private
- Send a nudge with one tap
- Auto-alert if buddy hasn't logged anything in 24 hours
